Who We Are

Efficient Team Technical Services LLC (ايفيشنت تيم للخدمات الفنية ش.ذ.م.م) is a Dubai-based technical services company providing expert solutions for residential and commercial clients across the UAE. From AC cooling and electrical work to full interior refinishing and gypsum installations, we cover the complete spectrum of technical needs.

Our registered office is located in Khawaneej 2, Alttay, Dubai — right at the heart of the community we serve. Our team of certified, experienced technicians is dedicated to delivering work that meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency.

We believe that every home and every business deserves reliable, professional care. That's why we are committed to transparent communication, competitive pricing, and workmanship that speaks for itself — on every single job.
